How To Play
  • 1. To launch a firework please click on the corresponding labels at the bottom of the screen.
  • 2. To begin recording a show, click "Start Recording".
  • 3. To stop recording, click "Stop Recording".
  • 4. After recording a show, you may playback the show by clicking "Watch My Show".
  • Warning, your previous show will be deleted upon clicking "Start Recording".
